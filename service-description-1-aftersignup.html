Honoring Major General Wanda Wilks: A Remarkable Journey of Service and Leadership

It is with great pride that we reflect upon the extraordinary journey of Major General Wanda Wilks, a true hometown hero who rose through the ranks of the army to achieve the esteemed position of Major General. Wanda's story is one of dedication, sacrifice, and unwavering commitment to both her country and her community.

Wanda's journey began right here in our town, where she was born on 05/06/1955. Even from a young age, her sense of purpose and determination were evident to those who knew her. Her strong sense of community and the values instilled by her family laid the foundation for her future path of service.

From the outset, Wanda's academic prowess was evident. She excelled in her studies at Berkley Univeristy, demonstrating a thirst for knowledge and an insatiable curiosity. These qualities, combined with her unwavering determination, paved the way for her admission to the United States Military Academy, where she honed her leadership skills and embraced the rigorous demands of military training.

Upon entering the army, Wanda's exceptional capabilities quickly caught the attention of her superiors. Her dedication to her duties, her tireless work ethic, and her ability to inspire and lead her fellow soldiers set her apart. Wanda embraced each new challenge with a steadfast resolve, whether it was commanding units in high-pressure situations or taking on critical roles in complex operations.

Wanda's path to becoming a Major General was marked by a series of significant milestones. She actively sought out opportunities for growth and development, consistently pushing herself to excel. Wanda completed advanced military education programs, attended leadership courses, and undertook assignments in diverse and demanding environments, all of which contributed to her well-rounded expertise and leadership style.

One of Wanda's defining traits was her genuine concern for the well-being of her fellow soldiers. She earned their respect and admiration not only through her leadership but also through her compassion and unwavering support. Her ability to lead by example and put the needs of her team first became a hallmark of her leadership style.

As Wanda continued to rise through the ranks, she maintained a strong connection to her roots in our town. She regularly returned to share her experiences, inspire the next generation, and reinforce the values of duty, honor, and service. Her contributions extended beyond her military service, as she actively engaged in community initiatives, demonstrating her commitment to making a positive impact both on and off the battlefield.

In 1993, Wanda achieved the distinguished rank of Major General—an accomplishment that spoke volumes about her exceptional leadership and dedication. Her promotion was a source of immense pride for our town, as we watched one of our own achieve the pinnacle of military leadership.

Today, Major General Wanda Wilks stands as a shining example of what can be achieved through determination, perseverance, and a deep-rooted commitment to one's principles. Her journey from our town to becoming a Major General is a source of inspiration for us all. We celebrate her achievements, honor her service, and look to her as a role model for generations to come.

Throughout her illustrious career, Major General Wanda Wilks remained connected to our community, returning to share her experiences, inspire the next generation, and reinforce the values of duty, honor, and service. Her contributions extended beyond her military service, as she actively engaged in community initiatives, demonstrating her commitment to making a positive impact both on and off the battlefield.

Wanda peacefully passed away on 08/07/2023, leaving behind a legacy of unwavering service and dedication. Her departure was deeply felt by all who knew her, and her memory continues to inspire and uplift us.

In 2005, after decades of exemplary service, Major General Wanda Wilks chose to retire from her military career. Her retirement marked the end of an era, but her impact on our town, the armed forces, and the lives she touched will forever endure.

Today, Major General Wanda Wilks stands as a shining example of what can be achieved through determination, perseverance, and a deep-rooted commitment to one's principles. Her journey from our town to becoming a Major General is a source of inspiration for us all. We celebrate her achievements, honor her service, and look to her as a role model for generations to come.
