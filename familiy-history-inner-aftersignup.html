Our Guardian Angel: Wanda Wilks

My mother, Wanda, was an extraordinary woman who embraced the role of motherhood with unwavering love and dedication. She graced this world in 1955, and after 68 years of showering our lives with warmth and affection, she peacefully passed away on 08/07/2023. As her son, I am honored to share the story of her remarkable journey.

From my earliest memories, Wanda radiated a nurturing spirit and a fierce determination that became the cornerstone of her life. She was a tireless learner, always seeking to expand her horizons and delve into new realms of knowledge. This insatiable thirst for learning shaped her into a lifelong scholar, devouring countless books and engaging in thought-provoking conversations.

Wanda's professional journey was equally inspiring. She approached her career with unwavering commitment, steadily climbing the ladder of success in her chosen path. Her skills and expertise were only matched by her compassion and unwavering ethics. Through her work, she touched the lives of many, leaving an indelible mark on all those she encountered.

As a mother, Wanda was the anchor that steadied our ship and the light that illuminated our way. She imparted invaluable life lessons, instilling in us the values of honesty, compassion, and perseverance. Her steady guidance and unconditional love formed the bedrock of our family's unity.

Among the many facets that defined Wanda, her love for nature and the great outdoors shone brilliantly. She found solace in the beauty of the natural world and frequently whisked us away on memorable escapades in nature. Whether it was camping trips beneath the stars, exploring rugged trails in the mountains, or simply basking in the serenity of a park, these moments etched themselves into our souls, becoming cherished memories we will forever hold dear.
